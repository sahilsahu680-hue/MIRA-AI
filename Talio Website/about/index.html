Our Story

From Vision to Reality

Talio was born from a simple observation: workforce management was broken. Teams were drowning in spreadsheets, struggling with outdated systems, and losing valuable time to administrative tasks that should have been automated.

As a venture of Mushroom World Umbrella, we had access to world-class resources and a legacy of innovation spanning over 15 years. MW Umbrella's mission to empower businesses and unlock creative potential became the foundation upon which Talio was built.

Today, Talio serves hundreds of companies worldwide, helping them streamline operations, boost productivity, and create better workplaces for their teams.
