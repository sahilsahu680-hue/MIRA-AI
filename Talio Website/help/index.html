To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address and you'll receive a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password. If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder.

Talio uses geofencing technology to verify employee location during check-in/check-out. Administrators can set up office locations with a specific radius. When employees attempt to check in, the app verifies their GPS coordinates are within the defined boundary. This ensures accurate attendance tracking without manual verification.

Yes! Talio is available on web browsers, Android devices, and as a desktop application. Your account syncs across all devices in real-time. However, for security purposes, attendance check-ins are typically restricted to your primary mobile device as configured by your administrator.

To apply for leave: 1) Navigate to the Leave section from the dashboard or sidebar. 2) Click "Apply Leave" button. 3) Select the leave type, start date, and end date. 4) Add a reason for your leave (optional but recommended). 5) Submit your request. Your manager will be notified and can approve or reject the request.

MIRA is Talio's AI-powered assistant that can help you with various tasks through natural language. You can ask MIRA to check your leave balance, submit attendance corrections, find company policies, get payroll information, and much more. Simply type or speak your question, and MIRA will provide instant answers or perform actions on your behalf.

Overtime is automatically calculated based on your organization's policies set by the administrator. Typically, hours worked beyond your standard shift duration (e.g., 8 hours) are tracked as overtime. The overtime rate (1.5x, 2x, etc.) and eligibility rules are configured in the system settings. You can view your overtime hours in the attendance reports section.

To download your payslip: 1) Go to the Payroll section from your dashboard. 2) Select the month and year for which you need the payslip. 3) Click the "Download" or "Print" icon next to the relevant payslip. 4) The payslip will be downloaded as a PDF file that you can save or print.
